004 Process
I
Immersion

Deep-dive into brand context, market landscape, and audience psychology — before a single mark is drawn. Understanding precedes creation.

II
Architecture

Building the visual logic: grids, type hierarchy, colour language, motion grammar. A system that scales without losing its soul.

III
Refinement

Iterating until nothing can be removed. The work is done not when there's nothing left to add, but when subtraction breaks it.
